#ICST2017

Warm greeting from Universitas Gadjah Mada Indonesia. The organising committee of the International Conference on Science and Technology 2016 (ICST2016) are pleased to invite you to present your works in the conference. This conference is the series of Universitas Gadjah Mada Annual Scientific Conference. The topics covered in the conference include, but not limited to, bio-prospecting, computer science, nanosciences and chemistry, physics and its applications and also geo maritime. The first International Conference on Science and Technology (ICST2015) was a success with more than 240 research papers and the accepted papers have been submitted to the AIP Proceeding for publication.

The ICST2016 is organised by Universitas Gadjah Mada and partner institutes and will be held in Yogyakarta, the centre for the Javanese culture. The aim of the conference is to provide a platform to the researchers and practitioners from both academia as well as industry to meet and share cutting-edge progress in the fields of science and technology. Graduate students, especially PhD students, are welcomed to submit their thesis works.

This year we will organise five symposia that are Bio-prospecting, Computer Science, Nanosciences and Chemistry, Physics and Its Applications and Geo Maritime. Please refer to the symposia information below to see the format of the submitted paper and its topics.

The submitted paper will be reviewed by at least two reviewers to ensure the scientific quality. The accepted papers will be published in the journals and proceedings that are indexed by Scopus i.e. Biological Research Journal (Pagepress), IEEE Xplore digital library, Trans Tech Publisher, Indonesian Journal of Chemistry, AIP Proceeding* and Indonesian Journal of Geography.
