OMICS: from Genomics to Metabolomics

Topics

Genomics, proteomics, transcriptomics, systems biology, lipidomics, fluxomics, chemical profiling, metabolomics (clinical, nutritional, therapeutic), proteogenomics, protein interactions, and activity-based proteomics.

Publication

Selected papers will be published in journals indexed by Scopus and DOAJ.
